SafeDrive - Arduino Based GPS Tracker with Accident Detection and Driver Attention Detection

Duration: Apr 2024 - Apr 2024

Associated with: National Institute of Technology Delhi

SafeDrive is an Arduino-based advanced driver assistance system designed to enhance road safety. It integrates GPS tracking to provide real-time vehicle location, accelerometer-based driver attention monitoring to detect inattentiveness, and automatic emergency notification capabilities. The system aims to ensure prompt assistance in the event of a road accident, especially in remote locations or adverse weather conditions where response times may be delayed. In case of a crash, it sends an SMS with the vehicle's GPS coordinates and a pre-programmed emergency message to selected contacts.

Skills: Internet of Things (IoT), Arduino, Microcontrollers, Project Management, Circuit Design, Creativity and Innovation

Racing FPV Drone Development for Technoxian World Robotics Championship

Duration: May 2024 - Aug 2024

Associated with: VCR Laboratory, National Institute of Technology Delhi

The Racing FPV Drone was developed as part of the Technoxian World Robotics Championship, where I worked under the VCR Laboratory team and received partial funding from NIT Delhi. The project aimed to create a high-performance racing drone for FPV (First Person View) racing, with an emphasis on speed, agility, and precision. Throughout the development, we focused on fine-tuning the drone’s hardware and software, ensuring that every component—from the flight controller to the FPV camera—was optimized for racing conditions. Although the project was a valuable learning experience, the drone was unfortunately destroyed due to a defective battery, highlighting the importance of quality control and testing in drone development. Despite this setback, the project provided hands-on experience with drone aerodynamics, embedded systems, and racing technologies.

Skills: Drone Development, FPV Racing, Embedded Systems, Circuit Design, Hardware Optimization, Project Management, Problem-Solving


LinearReg Billing & Inventory System in C

Duration: Nov 2023 - Nov 2023

Associated with: National Institute of Technology Delhi
